Kids Martial Arts in Hua Hin

Build confidence and discipline through our specialized youth program at Draco Fight Camp. We provide a safe, supportive environment for children to learn technical foundations and respect. It is the perfect outlet for energy and growth for families in Nong Kae.

Standard Class

Coordination & Balance

Every session starts with fun, movement-based drills designed to build your child's athletic coordination. We focus on fundamental body mechanics that improve balance and strength, providing a solid physical foundation for all sports and activities.

Non-Violent Defense

We teach kids how to use technical BJJ and wrestling to stay safe. Our "Concept-First" approach ensures they understand how to neutralize conflict through movement and control, prioritizing safety and intelligence over aggression or force.

Discipline & Respect

Martial arts is about more than just movement. We cultivate an ego-free environment where children learn the value of listening, following instructions, and respecting their peers, building vital social skills in the Hua Hin countryside.

Frequently Asked Questions

Click the drop down to learn more

What is the minimum age to start?

We generally welcome children starting from age seven. Our program is designed to be age-appropriate, focusing on engagement and basic coordination for younger students while gradually introducing more technical martial arts concepts as they grow and mature in Nong Kae.

Will my child get hurt?

Safety is our absolute priority. All kids' sessions are strictly supervised in a controlled environment. We emphasize "Technical Play" over hard contact, ensuring your child learns the beauty of martial arts without the risks found in more aggressive sports.

Is it okay for girls and boys?

Yes. Our classes are fully inclusive and perfect for both boys and girls. Martial arts is a universal skill that builds confidence for everyone. We have a diverse group of young students training together in our Thailand academy.

How does it help with school?

The discipline and focus required on the mats directly translate to the classroom. By learning to set goals and respect instructors at Draco Fight Camp, children develop the mental fortitude and concentration needed to excel in their academic lives.
