About

Our Mission

Draco Fight Camp was founded to bring a high-level, technical approach to martial arts in Hua Hin. Our mission is to move beyond the rigid formalities of traditional combat sports and focus on natural human behavior, leverage, and mechanical efficiency.

We are dedicated to providing an inclusive, ego-free environment where anyone—from children to adults—can develop functional skills in BJJ and MMA. Whether your goal is self-defense, fitness, or joining our future competition team, we provide the technical roadmap to get you there.

Coach

Head Coach: Mikael Forsgren

Mikael Forsgren is a 2nd Degree BJJ Black Belt with over 20 years of experience in high-level combat sports. Originally from Sweden, he established Draco Fight Camp as the premier technical academy in Hua Hin.

His coaching methodology focuses on mechanical efficiency and structural integrity. By prioritizing technical mastery over brute force, Mikael provides a professional roadmap for students in Nong Kae to reach their full potential on the mats

Our Philosophy

Control Your Movement

We prioritize technical mastery over brute force. Always respect your training partners, stay controlled during live rounds, and focus on leverage rather than using pure strength.

Consistent Effort

Progress in BJJ and MMA takes time and dedication. We encourage every student in Nong Kae to stay consistent, train hard, and enjoy the journey.

Leave the Ego Outside

Draco Fight Camp is a place for progress. We maintain a respectful, inclusive environment in Nong Kae where every student can focus on building real skills.

Facilities

Our gym is built for one thing: getting better. With a professional, full-sized octagon and a simple, effective layout, we provide the perfect workspace for anyone serious about improving their BJJ or MMA skills.

Located in the quiet hills of Hua Hin, the camp offers a calm and focused vibe. It is the ideal spot in Nong Kae to clear your head and put in the work.
