MMA

Mixed Martial Arts in Hua Hin

Experience a complete combat system that integrates striking, wrestling, and clinch work. Our MMA classes emphasize functional movement and real-world application within our professional octagon. Develop a versatile skillset in the peaceful Nong Kae countryside under expert technical guidance.

Standard Class

Striking Foundations

Every session starts with technical striking drills designed to build your coordination and power. We focus on the fundamental mechanics of boxing and kickboxing, ensuring you develop a sharp, effective stand-up game for combat sports.

Wrestling & Clinch

We bridge the gap between striking and grappling with detailed wrestling and clinch work. You will learn how to transition seamlessly between ranges, using the octagon to control opponents and dictate the pace of training.

Live Application

Put your skills to the test with controlled, situational sparring. We prioritize a safe, technical environment where you can apply your MMA foundations against resistance, building real-world confidence at our professional academy in Thailand.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience?

Not at all. Our MMA program is structured to accommodate everyone from total beginners to experienced athletes. We focus on building your foundations from the ground up in a supportive, ego-free environment right here in the Hua Hin countryside.

What equipment is required?

For your first class, just bring comfortable athletic wear and a mouthguard. As you progress at Draco Fight Camp, we can recommend the best gloves and shin guards for your training needs in Nong Kae to ensure maximum safety.

Is MMA training dangerous?

Safety is our primary focus. All sessions are conducted under professional supervision in our full-sized octagon. We emphasize technical control and mutual respect, allowing you to learn the complexities of mixed martial arts without the high risk of injury.

How does MMA help with self-defense?

By training in all ranges of combat—striking, clinching, and grappling—you develop a realistic understanding of physical confrontation. Our "Straight Talk" approach ensures you learn functional skills that are applicable in real-world scenarios while staying fit in Hua Hin.
