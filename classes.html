Martial Arts Training in Hua Hin

Draco Fight Camp provides a professional environment for mastering Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and Mixed Martial Arts. Led by 2nd Degree Black Belt Mikael Forsgren, our classes focus on technical efficiency and real-world application for all skill levels. Whether you are looking to compete or simply improve your fitness and self-defense, our academy offers a structured roadmap to success. Join a respectful community dedicated to progress in the heart of the peaceful Hua Hin countryside.

Brazilian Jui Jitsu

Master the art of technical grappling through our structured BJJ program. We focus on leverage and efficient movement, allowing you to control opponents regardless of size in a respectful, high-level training environment in Hua Hin.
