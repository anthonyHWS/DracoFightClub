Brazilian Jiu Jitsu in Hua Hin

Master the art of technical grappling at Draco Fight Camp. Our BJJ program focuses on mechanical leverage and structural integrity, allowing you to control opponents regardless of size. Join our professional, ego-free community in Nong Kae to begin your journey.

Standard Class

Technical Warm-up

Every session begins with specific drills designed to build your grappling coordination. We focus on solo and partner movements that reinforce the body mechanics necessary for effective, high-level Brazilian Jiu Jitsu in a safe environment.

Technique Study

We break down specific positions and submission chains with technical precision. You will learn the 'why' behind every grip and transition, ensuring you understand the mechanical leverage required to control opponents on the mats.

Live Training

Put your skills into practice with controlled live rounds. We prioritize a technical, ego-free environment where you can test your techniques against resistance, building real-world confidence at our academy in the Hua Hin countryside.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be in shape to start?

Absolutely not. Our technical approach to BJJ is designed to use leverage over athleticism. We welcome students of all fitness levels, focusing on individual progress and sustainable training habits within our peaceful Thailand countryside gym setting in Nong Kae.

What should I wear for my first class?

For your first session, comfortable athletic wear like a T-shirt and shorts is perfect. While we offer traditional Gi training, we provide a supportive environment for all beginners in Hua Hin and can advise you on equipment as you progress.

Is Brazilian Jiu Jitsu safe for beginners?

Safety is our highest priority at Draco Fight Camp. All sessions are supervised by Mikael, a 2nd Degree Black Belt, ensuring a controlled environment where you can learn effectively without the ego or intensity found in typical "fight" gyms.

Can I just drop in for a single session?

Yes. We welcome travelers and martial arts enthusiasts passing through Hua Hin. We offer affordable Day Passes for 500 THB, allowing you to experience our high-level technical instruction and professional octagon facility without a long-term commitment or contract.
